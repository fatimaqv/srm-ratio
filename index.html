Linear Growth Rate (LGR)

Based on maximum tumor diameter (MTD).
Guided by international recommendations for active surveillance of small renal masses (SRM).
Typical growth rates: 0.3–0.5 cm/year under active surveillance.

⚠️ Potential pitfalls for unrealistic growth:
  • Measurement variability (different slice plane or phase).
  • Intratumoral hemorrhage or transient inflammation.
  • Segmentation or registration error between studies.

Interpretation thresholds: ≤0.5 cm/year = stable, 0.5–1.0 cm/year = slow, >1.0 cm/year = accelerated.
Biological plausibility should always be confirmed by clinical judgment.
